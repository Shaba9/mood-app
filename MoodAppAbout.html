Welcome and Use:

Welcome to the MoodApp! A place where all the uncertainty about your feelings will become clear.
Ever in a situation where you need to reassess your emotions, but aren’t sure where to start? Well, pull up the MoodApp, answer a few questions, and you will know your true emotions in a virtual instant!
About to make a post on social media sharing your feelings, but aren’t sure what they are exactly? The MoodApp can help with that!
Have a thousand and one different emotions running through your mind and want to pin it down to one? Get started with the MoodApp!
Want to make conversation with the person next to you, but not sure if they are in the right mood? Have them take the MoodApp test!
Don’t want to make eye contact where you are right now and need an excuse to be on your phone? Click on over to the MoodApp!
Just plain bored? MoodApp it up!

Inspiration:

Now that you’re sold on the MoodApp, let’s talk a little about the inspiration behind the app. After a couple of decades on this planet, the creator behind the app grew weary of the irrationality of people’s actions. After some serious speculation, it was deemed that this was perhaps due to the lack of touch between a person and his/her/their true emotions. Thus, the MoodApp was created!

Future Aspirations:

The MoodApp team hopes to grow by finding better ways of gathering more accurate data that correlates between the events of the day and an individual’s emotions. If you can think of any, or if you want to say what an awesome app this is and how it has turned your life around for the better, contact us here!
